# Code of Conduct

1. **Be respectful.** Treat everyone with professionalism and courtesy.
2. **No harassment.** Hate speech, insults, or discrimination are not allowed.
3. **Keep it constructive.** Focus on solutions, not personal attacks.
4. **Stay on topic.** No spam, irrelevant content, or disruptive behavior.
5. **Follow project rules.** Respect the repo guidelines and maintain a positive environment.

Violations may result in warnings or removal from participation.
